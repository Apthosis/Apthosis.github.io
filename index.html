The Ghost Of Tsushima




The year is 1274. Samurai warriors are the legendary defenders of Japan, until the fearsome Mongol Empire invades the island of Tsushima,
wreaking havoc and conquering the local population. As one of the last surviving samurai, you rise from the ashes to fight back.
But, honorable tactics won't lead you to victory. You must move beyond your samurai traditions to forge a new way of fighting
- the way of the Ghost -
as you wage an unconventional war for the freedom of Japan.
