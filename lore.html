The history


The core story of Ghost of Tsushima
revolves around the hero Jin Sakai,
as he goes in a transformational journey with
his desire to defend the Island of Tsushima, its people, and way of life,
from the invading armies of the Mongol empire.
To appreciate the adventure of Jin fully, we must look
into the history where the game borrows some of the
most important characters and plot points that will appear in it..
